Why Aren't My CVs Getting Me Interviews?

Like a lock requiring a specific key, each job role has its own unique CV requirements. Nowadays, employers rely on automated applicant tracking systems to assess CVs against the skills and keywords specified in the job description. It has never before been so easy for employers to reject a multitude of CVs, shutting the door in the faces of countless qualified candidates, simply because of misaligned words in their documents.

Simply put, your prospective career now lies in the hands of a computer!
A good product with bad marketing won't sell.

Camouflage Your CV/Resume With the Job Description

Rather than limit your career opportunities to a small handful of jobs that match your existing CVs, why not pursue the jobs you really want by blending your CVs in accordingly?
This is where PaperChameleon comes in!

Use this service to tailor your CV for a high ATS score to bring you one step closer towards that dream job you worked so hard to land!

How the Process Works

  1. Email patrick.rolincin.bambi@gmail.com with a copy of your draft CV and a link to the job description. Be sure to include PaperChameleon in the subject line!

  2. The draft CV copy will be ATS scanned and a quote will be provided to you, with the price factoring the numbers of ATS-identified skills and keywords to match, as well as the reformatting workload.

  3. Once you accept the quote, specific payment instructions will be provided, most likely involving an international bank wire, which can be done through your local bank online (advanced) or in branch (beginner). Either way, your local bank may charge you a fee of about £25 for the international wire. This is separate from the fee charged by PaperChameleon for the CV optimisation service.

  4. Upon receiving proof of the wire, the CV optimisation shall commence. Although ATS is used to identify the buzzwords, the process of adding them to the CV in coherent sentencing along with reformatting is done manually. The new CV will be proof-read so it makes grammatical sense when assessed by human resources.

  5. You will be emailed with a copy of the new CV as a Microsoft Word document (should you wish to make any additional tweaks to the CV yourself), along with the ATS reports.

Prices

Depending on the number of skills, experience and keywords demanded by the job description and the complexity of reformatting, the price per CV can vary from as little as £20 to as high as £150. To minimise the costs on your end, it is recommended for you to work on your draft CVs to the best of your ability before sending them over (ensure that they have a coherent structure, although avoid the use of flashy colours or graphics).

Offers

Should you order a bundle of multiple CVs to optimise, PaperChameleon caters to that too, and with bulk discounts on the total price available. Bundle orders are recommended to reduce the proportional wiring fee charged by your local bank per CV. The discounts are as follows:

  • No Discount: 1 to 4 CVs

  • 10% Off: 5 to 9 CVs

  • 15% Off: 10 to 19 CVs

  • 20% Off: 20 CVs or more

By default, the CVs will be worked on in ascending order of later job application deadlines and the completed versions along with the corresponding ATS reports will be sent to you one by one as opposed to all at once, to buy you time to apply for the earlier jobs whilst the other CVs are worked on.
